Who is Gingy?

Gingy is a Gingerbread person created by the Muffin Man. He made his first appearance in the film, Shrek (2002): biscuit brown, white icing, gumdrop buttons, and that damn smile.

According to Wikipedia, while gingerbread and figurative biscuitmaking dates back between the 15th and 16th century, the court of Elizabeth I of England were witnesses to the first documented instance of figure-shaped gingerbread biscuits. The cookies were shaped and presented in the likeness of some of her important guests which brought the humanoid shape of the gingerbread.
